Refunds

숨은 돈 찾기 단계별 가이드

Many people leave small amounts of money unused because they do not know where to check. Hidden refunds can come from card points, tax adjustments, utility overpayments, insurance adjustments, or inactive accounts.

Start with common sources

Begin with card points, bank accounts, tax refund portals, health insurance adjustments, and utility providers. Keep a simple list of each place you checked and the date.

Use official sources first

Always start from official websites or trusted institutions. Avoid entering personal information into unfamiliar pages that promise instant money.

Make it a monthly habit

Checking once a month is enough for most people. The goal is not to chase every promotion, but to avoid losing money that already belongs to you.

Tip: Save screenshots or confirmation numbers when you submit a refund request.
