Subscriptions

Monthly Subscription Audit Checklist

Subscriptions are convenient, but they can quietly grow into a fixed monthly burden. A simple monthly review can reduce waste without affecting essential services.

List every recurring payment

Check bank statements, card statements, app stores, telecom bills, cloud storage, streaming services, and software tools.

Sort into three groups

Keep, cancel, and review later. If a service was not used in the last 30 days, it should be reviewed carefully.

Set a reminder

Do a subscription check once a month. Keep the process simple so it becomes a habit.
