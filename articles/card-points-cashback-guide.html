Cards & points

카드 포인트와 캐시백 활용법

Card points and cashback benefits are easy to forget. Many rewards have expiration dates or usage rules, so checking them regularly can prevent wasted value.

Check expiration dates

Log in to your card issuer or payment app and look for reward balance, points, cashback, coupons, and scheduled expiration.

Compare value before using points

Some points are better used for statement credit, while others may be more valuable for travel, shopping, or partner discounts.

Avoid spending more to earn rewards

Rewards are useful only when they reduce money you already planned to spend. Do not buy unnecessary items just to collect points.
